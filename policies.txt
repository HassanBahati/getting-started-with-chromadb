Return Policy: We offer a 30-day return window for all standard items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Returns are free for standard shipping.

Customized Items Policy: Customized, personalized, or monogrammed items cannot be returned or exchanged due to their unique nature. This includes engraved items, custom sizes, and personalized gifts.

Refund Process: Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned item. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase.

Exchange Policy: We offer exchanges for standard items within 30 days of purchase. Exchanges are subject to availability and may require additional payment if the new item costs more.

Damaged Items: If you receive a damaged item, please contact customer service within 48 hours of delivery. We will provide a prepaid return label and process a full refund or replacement.

Holiday Return Policy: During the holiday season (November 1 - January 31), we extend our return window to 60 days for standard items. Customized items remain non-returnable.

International Returns: International customers have 14 days to initiate returns. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was damaged or incorrect.

Clearance Items: All clearance and sale items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

Gift Returns: Gift recipients can return items within 30 days with a gift receipt. Without a gift receipt, store credit will be issued for the current selling price.

Warranty Information: All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Customized electronics are covered by warranty for manufacturing defects only, not for customization issues.
